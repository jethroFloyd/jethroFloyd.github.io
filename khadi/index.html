Vision

Culture

We bring to you products from the most culturally and aesthetically significant corners of the world.

The Green Touch

Everything we do is made by a human being. No chemicals and no heavy machinery. Everything you get is handwoven, handstitched and handfinished.

Unique

Here, we celebrate uniqueness. No two products are the same - and they infuse the best of the ancient, exotic heritage with the best contemporary and urban designs, personalized just for you - because you deserve it.

Motivation

Green Brand

Our products are made from the philosophy of three zeroes: Zero emission, Zero environmental burden and Zero water wastage.

Creating Smiles

We employ dexterous artisans from the bottom of the pyramid, and we provide them with a better, dignified life.

Conserving Culture

By using cutting-edge contemporary design philosophies coupled with heritage, we conserve as well as evolve culture.

Impact

Building Societies

By employing artisans from the rural, disjoint sections of the world. we give them a better life, dignity and stable employment - which leads to better standards of life.

Empowering Women

We focus especially on empowering women, because they hold the social fabric together.

Conserving Culture

We reinvent geo-indicators and materials that are intricately linked to a society, and we bring them to the fore of the world.
