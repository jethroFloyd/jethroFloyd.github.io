welcome

Hey there!

Here you might get some information about my work, interests and opinions. I have lived in places as esoteric as possible. I grew up in Kolkata in India - the city where you can spend hours on poetry and philosphy with tea. In Delhi, I found juxtaposition - you can walk from Chandni Chowk stuck in the era of the Taj Mahal to the blinding ultra-glamorous Connaught Place. Patna is a city mired in history - from being the capital of the great King Ashoka to the land of one of the oldest universities in the world - Nalanda. And then, you have London - the city where you will find charm blending in with speed.
I like listening to ideas and opinions. The adda spirit is integral to my existence! I am open to anything, from minesweeper to football, dubstep to classical and from python to cuda. I am currently studying Computer Science and Engineering at IIT P, India.
